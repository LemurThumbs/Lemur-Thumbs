Privacy Policy for Capyweather Last updated: July 21, 2026 Capyweather ("the App," "we," "us") is a simple weather widget developed for Android. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information. Information We Collect Capyweather requests access to your device's location data (GPS or approximate network-based location) solely to determine your current position and fetch the corresponding local weather forecast. This location data is used only in real time to make a request to our weather data provider and is not linked to your identity, name, or any account, since the App does not require or support account creation.policyforge How Location Data Is Used and Stored Your location coordinates are sent directly from your device to Open-Meteo's weather API to retrieve forecast data for that location; this request happens client-side, with no backend server operated by us in between. The App stores your last-used or selected location locally on your device (for example, as a cached setting) so the widget can display weather without asking for location every time; this locally stored data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party beyond the necessary weather API request.open-meteo Third-Party Services The App uses Open-Meteo, a free, open-source weather API, to retrieve forecast data based on the location coordinates you provide. Open-Meteo states that its website does not intentionally collect personal information, usage statistics, or other data that could identify users. No other third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks are integrated into Capyweather — the App contains no ads and does not track usage behavior.open-meteo Data Sharing We do not sell, rent, or share your location data with advertisers, analytics companies, or any other third parties. The only data transmission that occurs is the location coordinate sent to Open-Meteo to fetch weather results, which is required for the App's core function.policyforge Data Retention and Deletion Since Capyweather has no backend server and no user accounts, we do not retain any of your data on our servers. Any locally cached location or settings data stored on your device can be permanently deleted at any time by clearing the App's data or storage through your device's Android settings, or by uninstalling the App. Children's Privacy Capyweather does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App does not require sign-up, account creation, or the input of any personal identifiers. Your Choices You can revoke location permission at any time through your device's Settings, though doing so will limit the App's ability to automatically detect your local weather. Since no account or personal profile exists within the App, there is no stored personal data to request, edit, or export. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in the App's functionality or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
